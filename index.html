Bowl Bowl Mo!

Our JaPANIS ramen consists of noodles made from a dough of wheat flour, water, and salt, two halves of a soy sauce simmered boiled egg. The noodles also have other toppings like narutomaki fishcake made of ounces non-oily white fish filets, such as Alaska pollock or southern blue whiting, an egg white, a teaspoon of salt, a teaspoon of sugar, a teaspoon of mirin, and red food coloring, as well as chopped up spring onions, sliced beef, wakame seaweed, and sweet corn. (Toppings come in a seperate packet.)
